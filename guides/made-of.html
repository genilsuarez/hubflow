Made Of / From / With

Four ways to talk about materials and ingredients — the difference is whether you can still "see" the original substance.

made of
The material is still visible/recognisable
This table is made of wood. • This ring is made of gold.
made from
The material is transformed, no longer recognisable
Wine is made from grapes. • Paper is made from wood pulp.
made with
Lists one ingredient among several
This cake is made with fresh eggs.
made out of
Emphasizes creative/improvised use of material
She built a sculpture out of newspapers.
