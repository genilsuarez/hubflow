Relative Clauses

Relative pronouns add extra information about a noun without starting a new sentence.

PronounRefers toExample
whoPeople (subject)The man who called...
whichThingsThe book which is on the table...
whosePossessionThe woman whose car was stolen...
wherePlacesThe house where I grew up...
whenTimesThe day when we met...
