Plural Endings

The plural -s isn't always pronounced the same way — it depends on the sound right before it.

/s/
After voiceless consonants: p, t, k, f
cats • books • cups • months
/z/
After voiced consonants and vowels
dogs • cars • boys • dreams
/ɪz/
After sibilants: s, z, sh, ch, ge
buses • watches • judges • roses
