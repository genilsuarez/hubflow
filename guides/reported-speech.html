Reported Speech

When you report what someone said, the tense usually moves one step into the past — and time/place words change too.

⏳ Tense Backshift

Direct speechReported speech
"I am tired"She said she was tired
"I am working"He said he was working
"I have finished"He said he had finished
"I will call"She said she would call
"I can swim"She said she could swim
"I must leave"She said she had to leave

🕑 Time & Place Words Change Too

DirectReported
todaythat day
tomorrowthe next day
yesterdaythe day before
herethere
nowthen
💡 Why the change?
Reported speech is usually said later and/or in a different place, so words that pointed to "here and now" have to point to "there and then" instead.
