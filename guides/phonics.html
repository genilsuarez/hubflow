📈 Word Stress Rules

Common Stress Patterns
Oo (1st syllable)
Most 2-syllable NOUNS & ADJECTIVES
TAble • HAPpy • WAter • YELlow • DOCtor • NEver
oO (2nd syllable)
Most 2-syllable VERBS
beGIN • deCIDE • forGET • arRIVE • rePEAT • alLOW
Ooo (1st syllable)
Words ending in -ly, -ful, -ness
BEAUtiful • WONderful • HAPpiness • Usually
oOo (2nd syllable)
Words ending in -tion, -sion, -ic
eDUcation • fanTAStic • deCIsion • iMAgine
💡 Noun vs Verb stress shift
Same spelling, different stress: REcord (noun) vs reCORD (verb) • PREsent (noun) vs preSENT (verb) • OBject (noun) vs obJECT (verb)

🤐 Silent Letters

k
Silent before N
know • knife • knee • knock • knight
w
Silent before R
write • wrong • wrap • wrist • wreck
b
Silent after M / before T
climb • lamb • bomb • doubt • debt
h
Silent at start (common words)
hour • honest • honor • heir
g
Silent before N
gnat • sign • foreign • design • gnome
l
Silent in common words
walk • talk • half • calm • could • should
t
Silent in -sten, -stle
listen • fasten • castle • whistle • Christmas
p
Silent before S, N (Greek origin)
psychology • pneumonia • receipt
