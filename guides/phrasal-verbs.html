💡 Separable vs Inseparable
Separable: Object can go between verb & particle — "turn it off" or "turn off the light"
Inseparable: Object MUST go after the particle — "look after the kids" (NOT "look the kids after")
Rule: When the object is a pronoun (it, them, him, her), separable verbs MUST split — "pick it up" (NOT "pick up it")
